Complete the Metropolitan School of Data Census

Do you use data to improve people's lives? Good, we need your voice.


New York City's area data practice is older and longer than Gandalf's graybeard. Our part of the country contains its fair share of data wisdom and particularities. Criss crossing decades, formats, islands, counties, philosophies, municipalities, and states, our metro area has more data portals, more APIs, more CRMs, more data scientists & statisticians than any where else in the nation. The New York City Metropolitan School of Data is our way to discuss data in the service of people.

If you use data and work to improve lives in the New York City metro area, then you are part of the Metropolitan School of Data. Over the next year, BetaNYC is developing a number of programs to increase open data usability. Before we begin, we need to know who is out there and what you want. Then, in early 2016, we will bring together the metro area's data users and host a two day conference.
